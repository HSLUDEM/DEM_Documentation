Oil Boiler Central Plant
=======================================

The oil boiler central plant is a large-Scale
oil boiler attached to the district heating network.
It converts oil into useful heat.



.. include:: ../../../how_to_use_the_model/input_csv_as_rst/oil_boiler_cp.rst
